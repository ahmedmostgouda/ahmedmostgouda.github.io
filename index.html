Ahmed Gouda

Ahmed is a machine learning scientist in the field of medical imaging applications, with over 6 years of research and academic experience in Computer Vision, Signal Processing, Machine/Deep Learning, and Embedded Systems. He received an Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree in Medical Imaging and Applications (MAIA), awarded with a fully-funded grant from EMJMD to pursue this degree at University of Bourgogne (uB) - France, University of Cassino and Southern Lazio (UNICAS) - Italy, and University of Girona (UdG) - Spain. He has completed his master’s thesis at Canon Medical Research Europe Ltd. He holds B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Electronics and Communications Engineering from Arab Academy for Science, Technology and Maritime Transport (AASTMT) - Egypt.
